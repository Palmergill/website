My Projects

    Finance101


    This is an early prototype of a game I am making. I find finance and econmomics very interesting and have been investing in the stock market since I was 19. Personal fincance can be intimidating to some, so I wanted to create a game where people could learn some of the basics. Hopefully people will be able to have fun while they are learning, and not have to worry about losing real money. I am using the swift programming language in Xcode to make this app.
